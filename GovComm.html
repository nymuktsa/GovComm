Our First Project

As an off-shoot of the then Bronx Social Center, The Yam's first project was, in true Bronx fashion, a protest and boycott. As the movement died out, The Yam originally existed as an archive for the grassroots' activities. Eventually, it exploded in popularity, causing the Smiths and other various founders, like the newly elected NYC mayor, Zohran Mamdani, to expand out of the Bronx and NYC into other cities and regions.
